Dataset Overview

The dataset is sourced from the Kaggle Playground Series S4E2, containing obesity level estimations from Mexico, Peru, and Colombia. It includes 20758 records and 17 attributes.

Features:

  • Demographics: Gender, Age, Height, Weight
  • Eating Habits: FAVC, FCVC, NCP, CAEC, CH2O, CALC
  • Physical Conditions: SCC, FAF, TUE, MTRANS

Target Variable:

NObeyesdad (Obesity Levels): Underweight, Normal, Overweight, Obesity I, II, III.
